Getting Started

Based on: 01-exploring.md

Persona: Bob, 57, wants to track activities to improve his health.

Preconditions: Bob just installed the app on his phone.

Step 1: First Launch

Bob opens Sereus Health for the first time. He sees a list showing his recent log entries. Since he's new, there's a welcome message explaining how to get started.

Step 2: Empty State

If Bob had no entries yet, he'd see an encouraging message to add his first entry.

Step 3: Adding an Entry

Bob taps the (+) button to add a new entry. He's presented with a clean form to select what he wants to log.

Step 4: Selecting Items

Bob selects "Activity" → "Eating" and sees food items to choose from. He notices he can add new items if what he ate isn't listed.

What's Next?

Bob has successfully logged his first entry. He commits to using the app daily to track his activities, conditions, and outcomes.
