Configuring Catalog

Based on: 03-configuring.md

Persona: Bob wants to populate his catalog with all the items he tracks daily.

Preconditions: Bob has been using the app and knows what items he needs.

Step 1: Opening the Catalog

Bob taps the Catalog tab to manage his items and bundles. He sees a list organized by type.

Step 2: Empty Catalog

If Bob hadn't added anything yet, he'd see prompts to add his first items.

Step 3: Adding a New Item

Bob wants to add "Omelette" as a new food item. He taps (+) and fills in the details.

Step 4: Editing an Item

Bob taps on "Omelette" to edit it. He can change the name, category, or add quantifiers.

Step 5: Creating a Bundle

Bob creates a "BLT" bundle for logging his favorite sandwich quickly. He selects bacon, lettuce, and tomato.

Step 6: Editing a Bundle

Bob reviews his BLT bundle and adds bread to complete it.

What's Next?

With a well-organized catalog, Bob can log entries quickly. Now he wants to see trends in his data.
