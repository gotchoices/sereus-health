# Reminders

Based on: [08-reminders.md](../../stories/08-reminders.md)

**Persona**: Bob wants gentle reminders to log entries throughout the day.

**Preconditions**: Bob has been using the app but sometimes forgets to log.

---

## Step 1: Opening Settings

Bob taps the Settings tab to configure his preferences.

[![Settings](../images/settings.png)](health://screen/Settings)

---

## Step 2: Configuring Reminders

Bob taps "Reminders" and sees a simple interval picker. He sets it to remind him if he hasn't logged anything in 4 hours.

[![Reminders](../images/reminders.png)](health://screen/Reminders)

---

## Step 3: Receiving a Reminder

Later that day, Bob has been busy and hasn't logged lunch. His phone shows a notification: "Haven't logged in a while. Tap to add an entry."

Tapping the notification opens the app directly to the new entry screen.

[![New Entry](../images/edit-entry-new.png)](health://screen/EditEntry?mode=new)

---

## What's Next?

Bob now has a complete health tracking system:
- ✓ Easy entry logging with bundles
- ✓ Visual trends with graphs
- ✓ Secure data with Sereus network
- ✓ Gentle reminders to stay consistent

He's ready to discover insights that improve his health!
