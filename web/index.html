How it works

  1. Log activities, conditions, and outcomes — as quickly as a tap.
  2. Quantify what matters: intensity, duration, quantity, a 1–10 scale.
  3. Graph any items together over time to spot correlations.
  4. Share clear reports with your doctor or therapist.

Your data stays on your device. Sereus networking (planned) will let you sync and share with trusted parties on your terms.

Starter catalogs

A catalog is a ready-made set of categories and items to log against, so you don't start from a blank page. Download one and import it in the app (Catalog → Import), or start minimal and add your own.
